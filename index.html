          HOW IT WORKS

          • On touch screens, drag anywhere on the course to aim. With a mouse, drag back from the ball. Release to hit.
          • Pick a club before each shot — tap the chips below the course, or press 1 / 2 / 3 (C cycles).
          • Driver flies long and low. Wedge lofts high to clear sand and trees. Putter stays on the ground and is dead accurate.
          • Trees can be chipped over. Mountains and steel barriers cannot — route around them.
          • Carry the water in the air — land in the pond and it's a +1 penalty, replayed from where you last hit.
          • Nine hand-built holes combine split fairways, doglegs, forest shortcuts, water carries, and mountain routes. Fewest strokes wins.
          • Your best round is saved to a live leaderboard (Today + All-time), and you'll see when other players online finish.
