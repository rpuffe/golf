          HOW IT WORKS

          • On touch screens, drag anywhere on the course to aim. With a mouse, drag back from the ball. Release to hit.
          • Pick a club before each shot — tap the chips below the course, or press 1 / 2 / 3 (C cycles).
          • Power goes furthest but runs hot. Loft clears hazards and lands soft. Precision stays grounded and follows the line exactly.
          • Trees can be chipped over. Mountains and steel barriers cannot — route around them.
          • Water costs +1. Lava costs +2. Both replay from your last shot. Lofted balls can carry either.
          • Ice barely slows a rolling ball, so it can create huge banks or brutal overshoots.
          • Neon bumpers reflect your incoming angle and return a little speed. Loft can chip over them; ground shots can call a bank.
          • On Lucky Spiral, a hard ground shot locks into two icy laps. The final bounce has a chance to drop for an ace; Loft can safely cross the rails instead.
          • Nine escalating holes each hide a safe route and a dangerous scoring line. The final holes combine everything.
          • Your best round is saved to a live leaderboard (Today + All-time), and you'll see when other players online finish.
